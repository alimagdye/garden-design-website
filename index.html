About us

We are passionate about creating beautiful and sustainable gardens. Our team of experts is dedicated to bringing your vision to life.

Lucky Shrub is a medium-sized garden design firm that specializes in garden design and creation, maintenance and landscaping. The company also runs a small plant nursery that sells indoor and outdoor plants. Lucky Shrub was started by a husband and wife team, Jason and Maria, who share a long-time love for plants.

Jason is the garden architect. He creates and oversees all designs while managing his team of landscapers. Maria manages all the marketing for the company and oversees the nursery.
